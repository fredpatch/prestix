## Bugs Found And Fixed

- Proforma‚Üíinvoice promotion silently dropped `referrerPartyId` ‚Äî the r√©f√©rent picked on a proforma never carried forward to the resulting invoice. Real data-loss bug, not a display issue.
- Date-range boundary bug across every reporting query: `new Date(to)` parses as midnight UTC at the *start* of that day, so every `lte(column, to)` comparison was silently excluding same-day activity. Affected 8 separate query sites in `reporting.service.ts`. Fixed with a single shared `endOfDay()` helper ‚Äî now the only way `to` becomes a `Date` for a `lte` comparison anywhere in the module.
